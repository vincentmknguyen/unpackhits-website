// COMMON QUESTIONS

QUESTIONS?

Short answers. If yours isn't here, just use the form and ask.

What if my cards don't match what I entered?
It happens. I'll email you an adjusted offer based on what I actually received and pay out that amount. You'll be notified of any changes before PayPal goes out. I don't ship cards back.
Do you take graded cards?
Yes — use the "I also have graded cards or sealed product" checkbox at the bottom of the form. I'll follow up personally since these are priced individually.
Why a PO box?
Security and volume. I process a lot of packages and a PO box keeps everything organized.
How fast do I get paid?
Up to 5 business days after your package arrives — I verify the cards, then send PayPal. PayPal only for shipped orders. Cash on pickup for meetups.
What about sealed product?
Use the graded/sealed checkbox at the bottom of the form. Sealed is priced individually, so I'll follow up personally rather than quoting it through the calculator.
Do you ship outside the US?
US only for now.
What if my collection is under 4,500 cards?
Still welcome — $20 minimum offer. Shipping is on you for smaller collections. Or meet up in the Bay Area and skip shipping entirely.
